// Dimension tables in a cube and form star schema

1. Click a New Dimension in the Wizard to start the Dimension Wizard. Select the option to skip the welcome screen, and then click on Next.

2. Click the Option Star Schema: A Single Dimension Table, and the click Next.

3. Select SaleFact from Available Tables and click next

4. Select Time Dimension ->Select Date column From Drop Down : Mon. & Click Next 

5. Select Time Level : Year, Quater, Month ->Click Next.

6. Select all and click Next.

7. Give Dimension Name: TimeDimension.-> Click Finish.

Create one more dimension: StoreDimension.

8. Specify Dimension Level : StoreId, Store Name-> Click Next.

9. Select all and Click Next.

10. Select Yes.... -> Click Next.

11. Finally cube is created. -> Cube Name :Sales_Cube.
